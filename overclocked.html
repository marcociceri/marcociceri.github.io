Marco Ciceri
Overclocked
This video work presents real-life microscopic footage of supercomputers under physical strain as they perform intensive AI training. At a scale rarely seen, the film captures the invisible labor of machines—circuitry sweating, components pulsing, and heat dissipating through liquid cooling systems. What appears cold and computational reveals an uncanny sense of exhaustion, a mechanical body pushed to its limits. In magnifying this hidden choreography, the work invites viewers to reflect on the material and energetic toll of artificial intelligence.
