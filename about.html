Hello, my name is Marco I am an Italian visual artist based in Berlin. I am currently part of Pfadfinderei, a collective of designers and engineers specialized in creative services for stage entertainment, media installations and events. Previously I worked at Marshmallow Laser Feast in London and I graduated in design and arts at the University of Bolzano. For my animations, I often use automated movements created with generative software or physical motorized gears. I love to mix different media and techniques to achieve richness in the details and what I call a “flowing esthetic”.
