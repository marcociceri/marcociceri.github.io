Marco Ciceri

Hello, my name is Marco I am an Italian visual artist based in Berlin.
I work with light and video to create live shows and installations.
For five years I was part of Pfadfinderei, previously I worked at Marshmallow Laser Feast in London and I graduated in design and arts at the University of Bolzano.
For my animations, I often use automated movements created with generative software or physical motorized gears. I love to mix different media and techniques to achieve richness in the details and what I call a "flowing esthetic".
