Hüma Utku - Comfort of the Shadows

Videoclip for the single 'Comfort of the Shadows' part of the album 'Dracones' out on Editions Mego

This video piece explores the forgotten state of fetal existence—an intimate journey into the earliest moments of life. In a world where perception is primal, reduced to the most fundamental inputs: light and vital energy. The experience is alien yet universal, a return to a time before the self can be remembered or formed. The piece evokes the sensation of being without yet truly being, offering a glimpse into the forgotten interiority of our earliest experience.
