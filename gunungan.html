Gunungan - Live A/V with Lintang Radittya
Gunungan is a live A/V performed by the musician and instrument builder Lintang Radittya and the visual artist Marco Ciceri. The Gunungan is a figure in the Indonesian theatrical performance – the Wayang – representing different elements of Javanese mythology. The Gunangan performance is a modern interpretation of the Wayang theatre with the traditional puppets transformed into electronic instruments and mythological symbols projected in eclectic sequences.
Comissioned by Goethe-Institut Indonesien
